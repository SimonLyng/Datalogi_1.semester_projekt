Game Rules

The objective of the game is to guess the word by suggesting letters within a certain number of guesses.

  • Each player takes turns to guess a letter.
  • If the suggested letter exists in the word, it will appear in all its correct positions.
  • If the suggested letter does not exist in the word, one guess is subtracted.
  • The game continues until the word is completely guessed or all guesses are used.
